CLANN

Cerebellum-Like Artificial Neural Networks bio-inspired on cerebellum

Our Objective

Create new bio-inspired artificial neural network architectures in the cerebellum and cerebellar-like structures, combining efforts and expertise from various disciplines such as neuroscience, engineering, computer science, and neuro-ethics.

We aim to create an interdisciplinary and international research network focused on the development and evolution of the cerebellum and on cerebellar-inspired artificial neural networks.

Making Social Impact

The project contributes to society in the sense that it increases knowledge about biological neural networks, and uses this information to optimize the development of new artificial intelligence technologies. We also consider it is relevant for society the exploration of potential ethical issues that could arise from research and applications.

Our team

We are a global team with members located in different countries. Our collaborative efforts transcend geographical boundaries as we bring together computer engineers and neuroscientists from diverse backgrounds and cultures.
